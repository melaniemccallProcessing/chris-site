About

Built by students, for students who deserve a seat at the table.

Who We Are

Equal Path CRE was founded by Chris McCall, a Commercial Real Estate major at the University of Georgia, with the mission of creating more access and opportunity for students from underrepresented backgrounds.

What We Do

  • Education on commercial real estate and corporate finance
  • Networking opportunities across universities
  • Exposure to real career paths and opportunities
  • Biweekly virtual and in-person meetups

We're building the platform I wish existed when I started. One that gives every student—regardless of background—a real path into commercial real estate.

CM
Chris McCall
Founder · University of Georgia
